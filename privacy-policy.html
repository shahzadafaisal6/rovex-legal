01 Introduction

Rovex (“we”, “our”, “us”) is developed by Abbas and operates the mobile application Rovex (package name com.abbas.rovex) and related services. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, how we store and protect it, and what choices you have.

Important: Rovex offers community Caller ID, which may involve uploading contact names and phone numbers you choose to sync. We ask for your explicit consent in the app before you continue.

By using Rovex, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the app.

02 Information we collect

Account information

When you sign in with Google and verify your phone number, we may collect:

  • Display name and email address (from Google sign-in)
  • Phone number (for verification and Caller ID profile linking)
  • Firebase user ID and authentication tokens
  • FCM device token (for push notifications)

Contacts

With your permission, we read contact names and phone numbers on your device to:

  • Display names in call logs, SMS, and search
  • Sync contacts to our secure backend (Supabase) for Caller ID and directory features you enable

Call logs & phone activity

With permission, we access call history and phone state to show your call log, detect calls, provide Caller ID overlays, call screening, and spam blocking.

SMS & MMS

If you use Rovex as your SMS app or grant SMS permissions, we process message metadata and content on your device to display conversations, send messages, and optionally detect OTP codes for convenience. We do not sell SMS content.

Caller ID lookups

For unknown numbers, we may send a normalized phone number to our servers to retrieve a suggested caller name. Results may be cached locally on your device.

VoIP (in-app calls)

Voice and video calls through Agora use microphone and camera only during active calls you initiate or answer inside Rovex.

Notification metadata (optional)

If you enable Notification Access, we may read notification titles/text from WhatsApp call alerts to show caller names for WhatsApp calls. We do not access WhatsApp chat messages through this feature.

Accessibility service (optional)

If you manually enable Rovex under Android Settings → Accessibility, our Accessibility Service is used only for incoming WhatsApp and WhatsApp Business voice/video calls. While WhatsApp’s call screen is visible, we may read on-screen text (such as the caller name shown by WhatsApp) to display caller identification inside Rovex. We do not use Accessibility to read WhatsApp chats, groups, media, or content from other applications. You can disable this at any time in Accessibility settings.

Technical & usage data

  • Device type, OS version, app version
  • Crash logs and analytics (via Firebase Analytics, if enabled)
  • IP address and general network information when connecting to our servers

03 How we use your information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide dialer, SMS, Caller ID, spam blocking, and VoIP features
  • Authenticate your account and prevent abuse
  • Improve app performance, reliability, and security
  • Send service-related push notifications (e.g. incoming call or message alerts)
  • Comply with legal obligations where applicable

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

04 Sharing & service providers

We use trusted processors to operate Rovex:

  • Google Firebase — Authentication, Cloud Firestore, Cloud Messaging, Analytics, Storage
  • Supabase — Contact sync and Caller ID database (hosted infrastructure)
  • Agora — Real-time voice and video communication
  • Google Play Services — Sign-in and device services

These providers process data under their own terms and only as needed to deliver our features. We may also disclose information if required by law or to protect rights, safety, and security.

05 WhatsApp calls — Accessibility disclosure

Single purpose: Rovex’s Accessibility Service exists only to detect incoming WhatsApp / WhatsApp Business calls and show the caller name in Rovex. It is not used for ads, automation of other apps, or reading your messages.

What we access (only if you turn Accessibility on for Rovex):

  • Window content from com.whatsapp or com.whatsapp.w4b during an active call screen
  • Caller name or call-related labels shown on WhatsApp’s incoming call UI

What we do not access:

  • WhatsApp chat messages, status, or media galleries
  • Banking, social, or other apps via Accessibility
  • Keystrokes or passwords

Notification access (optional): As an alternative or supplement, you may enable Notification Access so Rovex can read WhatsApp call notifications only. Either or both features are optional; regular SIM Caller ID does not require them.

Data read from WhatsApp call screens or notifications is processed on your device to show Caller ID in Rovex and is not sold. Disable Accessibility or Notification Access anytime to stop this processing.

06 Device permissions

Rovex may request permissions including: contacts, phone, call log, SMS, camera, microphone, overlay (display over other apps), optional notification access, optional Accessibility (WhatsApp calls only), and battery optimization exemption. Each permission supports a visible feature described in-app before use. You can revoke permissions anytime in Android Settings.

07 Data retention

We retain account and synced data while your account is active and as needed to provide services. Local data on your device is removed when you uninstall the app. Server-side data is deleted or anonymized within a reasonable period after a verified deletion request, unless we must retain it for legal reasons.

08 Your rights & choices

Depending on your region, you may have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data
  • Withdraw consent (e.g. revoke permissions or stop contact sync)
  • Object to or restrict certain processing
  • Lodge a complaint with a data protection authority

To request account or cloud data deletion, contact the developer (see Contact section below) with your registered phone number and Google account email. We will respond within 30 days where possible.

09 Children

Rovex is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact us and we will delete it.

10 Security

We use industry-standard measures including HTTPS/TLS for data in transit and access controls on our backends. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security.

11 Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the new version on this page and update the “Last updated” date. Continued use after changes means you accept the updated policy.
